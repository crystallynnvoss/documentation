# Donate

You can contribute funding to support SPEC's mission on our [Open Collective Page](https://opencollective.com/spec/contribute). Open Collective \(OC\) is an online funding platform for open and transparent communities. The OC platform provides the tools to raise and share our finances in full transparency, making all our budget 100% open to the public. Donations to SPEC are tax-deductible, thanks to its fiscal sponsor, the [Open Collective Foundation](https://opencollective.foundation).

## Contributor Tiers

| Tier | Amount |
| :--- | :--- |
| Donation | Any  amount |
| Friend | $5 per month |
| Backer | $20 per month |
| Sponsor | $50 per month |
| Partner | $100 per month |
| Hero | +1,000 one-time contribution |
